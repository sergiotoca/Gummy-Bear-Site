Classic Gummy Bears Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup corn syrup
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 package (3 oz) flavored gelatin
  • 1 package (0.25 oz) unflavored gelatin
  • 1/4 tsp citric acid
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Gummy bear molds

Instructions

  1. Combine sugar, corn syrup, and water in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly until sugar dissolves.
  2. Add flavored gelatin, unflavored gelatin, and citric acid to the saucepan and stir until fully combined.
  3. Bring mixture to a boil and remove from heat.
  4. Add baking soda and stir well. The mixture will bubble and expand.
  5. Immediately pour the mixture into the gummy bear molds and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Remove gummy bears from the molds and enjoy!

Recipe

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 10 minutes
  • Total time: 50 minutes
  • Servings: 24
